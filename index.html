About केशर अमृततुल्य

A journey of tradition, taste, and trust that has transformed into India's most loved Gud Tea brand.

The Gud Tea Revolution

केशर अमृततुल्य was born from a simple yet powerful vision — to bring back the authentic taste of traditional Indian tea made with pure jaggery (gud). In a world of artificial sweeteners, we chose the path of purity and tradition.

What started as a single outlet has now grown into a nationwide phenomenon with 380+ franchise outlets across 12+ states. Our unique Gud Tea concept has resonated with millions of tea lovers who crave the authentic, wholesome taste of their childhood.

We are a registered brand with a proven business model, complete franchise support system, and a vision to make pure Gud Tea accessible to every corner of India.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our franchise opportunity.

What is the investment required for the franchise?+
The investment depends on your location and store size. We offer flexible investment options to suit different budgets. Contact our franchise team for a detailed investment breakdown tailored to your specific requirements.
How much space is required for the outlet?+
A minimum of 100 sq ft space is required for a standard outlet. However, we recommend 150-200 sq ft for optimal customer experience and operational efficiency. We also provide guidance on ideal location selection for maximum footfall.
Do you provide training and ongoing support?+
Yes, absolutely. We provide comprehensive training covering tea preparation, customer service, inventory management, and business operations. Additionally, you receive ongoing operational support, marketing assistance, and regular performance reviews.
What is the expected ROI timeline?+
While we cannot guarantee specific returns as business performance depends on location and operations, most of our franchise partners achieve operational breakeven within 6-12 months. We provide realistic projections based on your location during the consultation.
Can I operate the franchise alongside my current job?+
Yes, many of our franchise partners operate their outlets semi-passively by hiring a trained manager. Our simplified operations model makes it possible to manage the business with minimal daily involvement once properly set up.
What makes Gud Tea different from regular tea?+
Gud Tea uses pure jaggery (gud) instead of refined sugar. Jaggery is rich in minerals, aids digestion, and provides a more authentic, traditional taste. It's healthier, more natural, and appeals to the growing health-conscious consumer base.
Do you help with location selection?+
Yes, our team provides complete location analysis and selection support. We evaluate footfall, competition, demographics, and visibility to help you choose the best location for maximum success.
What is the franchise agreement duration?+
Our standard franchise agreement is for 5 years, with renewal options. We believe in long-term partnerships and offer favorable terms for renewals to successful franchise partners.
